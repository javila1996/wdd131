Next Level FC

Domain Idea: nextlevelfc.org

Next Level FC is a private soccer club aimed at youth athletes who are dedicated to improving their skills and competing at the next level. We chose this name because it clearly represents our mission: helping players reach the “next level” of competition, whether that's varsity, academy, or collegiate soccer.

Site Purpose

This website provides detailed information about our youth soccer club, including training camp schedules, try-out locations, cost details, coaching staff, and facilities. The site also features photo galleries of youth in training, staff, and our world-class facilities. It serves as a hub for young athletes and parents interested in taking their soccer development seriously.

Scenarios

  • What are the dates and costs for upcoming training camps?
  • Where will official try-outs be held, and how can I register?

Color Schema

  • Accent Green (#10B981): Used for call-to-action buttons, links, and highlights.
  • Primary Blue (#1F2937): Used for headings and navigation.
  • Light Gray Background (#F9FAFB): Used as the page background to maintain readability.

Typography

  • Montserrat (sans-serif): Used for headings to make them bold and distinct.
  • Open Sans (sans-serif): Used for body text to enhance readability and clarity.
